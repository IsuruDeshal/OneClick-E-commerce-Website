Privacy Policy

How we handle your personal information on One Click Computers.

1. Introduction

We value your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill orders

We use cookies for essential functionality and to analyze site usage. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

We use secure systems and follow best practices to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
